Dwayne Johnson

Life

Dwayne Johnson, also known as The rock, was born in Hayward, California, on May 2, 1972. He was raised as an only child but later found out that his father, Rocky Johnson, had 7 other kids with 6 different women. Growing up, Dwayne would watch his dad wrestle all the time, And started showing interest in the sport at age 11 inspired by Rocky johnson's success. 13 years later, when he was 24 years old he fought his first wrestling match. It was a close game but he ended up winning in the end.
